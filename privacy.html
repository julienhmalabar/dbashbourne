privacy statement

This privacy statement has been included in this website is in order to clarify how Ethypharm UK Limited handles personal data supplied by yourself in the process of using this website. Our data protection and privacy statement wording is required as a result of data protection law arising pursuant to a European Union data protection directive. Businesses in European Union countries must comply with national laws enacted in light of their country's interpretation of the EU data protection directive. The legislation seeks to ensure that all personal information held by businesses and organisations is both accurate and suitably protected in order to ensure there is no mis-use of personal data relating to an individual held by the business or organisation.

Accordingly, Ethypharm UK Limited endeavours to ensure that when processing your personal data we are protecting both our and your interests by complying with the relevant legislation.
Therefore, please carefully read the privacy statement below, which has been included with a view to protecting your personal data as well as ensuring that Ethypharm UK Limited complies with the relevant legislation.

This web site is provided by Ethypharm UK Limited.

When you visit this web site and in the course of our providing services to you, Ethypharm UK Limited may collect personal information about you directly when you are asked to provide data (e.g. name, e-mail address and mailing address) or when you email, phone, or otherwise contact, us.

The website does not collect information indirectly such as by cookies or any other technical means to monitor your use of the website.

We may process personal data collected via this web site for the purposes of administering the web site, responding to queries or providing services to you at your request, updating our records and databases, improving the personalisation of our services to you and maintaining contact data about you or a third party (for example, your employer).

The data may, for these purposes, be disclosed to certain types of staff within Ethypharm UK Limited’s organisation (i.e. staff whose job it is to process your enquiries and orders or otherwise to administer the web site, and those staff involved in the provision of our services which may be of interest to you). Personal data may also be disclosed to a limited number of third parties, to send you information which may be of interest. Any such information will be solely connected with the services and products of Ethypharm UK Limited.

We have adopted generally accepted standards of technology and operational security in order to protect the confidentiality and security of personal data collected via this web site but the nature of the Internet is of course such that the data may flow over networks without appropriate security measures and may be accessed and used by unauthorised persons.

Please note that this Privacy Statement only covers this web site and does not regulate the privacy practices of other web sites which may be accessible through or linked to this web site. We take no responsibility for the use of any information about you which is collected as a result of your use of these websites.

We do not seek to collect data about your racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or other similar beliefs, trade union membership, physical or mental health, sexual life, criminal record or alleged criminal activity through the web site ("sensitive personal data"). We may collect some sensitive personal data accidentally. By providing us with unsolicited sensitive personal data you consent to our using those data for the above purposes.

We reserve the right to revise this policy at any time.
